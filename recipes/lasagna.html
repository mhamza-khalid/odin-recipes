Lasagna Recipe

Description

Recipe

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that features layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with a variety of flavorful ingredients. It typically includes a rich meat sauce, such as Bolognese or marinara sauce, along with creamy cheese, such as ricotta or mozzarella. The layers are often topped with grated Parmesan cheese, which forms a golden crust when baked. The dish is then baked in the oven until the pasta is tender and the flavors meld together, creating a delicious and comforting meal. Lasagna is a versatile dish that can be customized with different fillings, making it a favorite for gatherings and family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Fresh basil

Recipe

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef or Italian sausage with diced onion and minced garlic in a skillet.
  3. Drain any excess fat from the meat and add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer the meat sauce for 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, and chopped fresh basil.
  6. Preheat the oven to a specified temperature (e.g., 375°F or 190°C).
  7. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for about 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Allow the lasagna to rest for a few minutes before serving.
  12. Garnish with additional fresh basil, if desired.
  13. Slice and serve the lasagna.
